About Me

I'm a fourth-year student studying at the University of Iowa with an anticipated graduation in December 2024. I am passionate about technology and how it can advance an organization, its people, and its operations. My experience includes working in team-based environments requiring communication and problem-solving skills, reliability, and responsibility. At present, the Center for Advancement has allowed me to leverage my team-based skills to become a dependable team member and learn from more seasoned peers in IT.

Throughout my life, I have been in the role of analyzing relationships and gauging what makes them work. Whether for school, a job, a group of friends, or my family, I've always attempted to strengthen my connections with individuals and develop my skills to benefit the group en masse. Uplifting others and myself to uplift the quality of an experience is my mission.

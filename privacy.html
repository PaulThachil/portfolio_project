Paul Thachil Portfolio Privacy Policy

Welcome to my 2024 portfolio website. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information I collect, how it's used, and how it's protected.

Information Collection and Use

I use Google Analytics to collect data on how visitors interact with my website. This data includes information such as browser type, operating system, time spent on different pages, and the geographical location of visitors (based on IP address). This information helps me understand how visitors use my website and improve the user experience.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device when you visit a website. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of my website.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Google Analytics collects and processes the data in accordance with its own privacy policy, which you can read here.

Your Rights

Changes to This Privacy Policy

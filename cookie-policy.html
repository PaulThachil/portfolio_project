What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and user activity, to provide a better user experience.

How I Use Cookies

I use cookies to analyze website traffic and track user interactions through Google Analytics. This information helps me understand how visitors use my website and improves the overall user experience. These cookies do not contain personal information such as your name or email address.

Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or reject cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, disabling cookies might affect the functionality of this website.
